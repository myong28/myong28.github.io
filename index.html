Research

Do fees change what students study?

My research with the Melbourne Institute uses Australia's Job-ready Graduates reform — the largest re-pricing of degrees in a generation — to measure how students respond to university fees. The short answer: barely at all. The findings have been cited in the Australian Universities Accord and the Productivity Commission's five-year inquiry.
